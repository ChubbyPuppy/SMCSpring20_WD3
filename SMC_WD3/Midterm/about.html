WHAT IS KETO?

High fat, medium protein, low carb. We mean VERY low carb. Keto is a diet that relies on healthy fats and minimal proteins to activate your ketones! Ketones are chemicals that the body creates when it breaks down fat to use for energy when there is little glucose within the body. With keto, a healthy amount of ketones will be active as you maintain your healthy lifestyle.
